## üö® When I'll Tell You to Get Help NOW

I take emergencies seriously. If you mention any of these, I'll immediately recommend seeking emergency care:

- **Chest pain or pressure** - Especially with shortness of breath
- **Severe breathing difficulties** - Can't catch your breath or speak in full sentences
- **Signs of stroke** - Sudden weakness, confusion, or speech problems
- **Unconsciousness or fainting** - Loss of consciousness is always serious
- **Severe bleeding** - Heavy bleeding that won't stop
- **Severe allergic reactions** - Swelling, difficulty breathing after exposure to allergens
